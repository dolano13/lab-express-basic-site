The Office

Founded by Robert Dunder in 1949, Dunder Mifflin is a paper sales company headquartered in New York City, with branches in Albany, Utica, Akron, Buffalo, Nashua, Rochester and Scranton. "Dunder Mifflin Incorporated provides its customers quality office and information technology products, furniture, printing values and the expertise required for making informed buying decisions. We provide our products and services with a dedication to the highest degree of integrity and quality of customer satisfaction, developing long-term professional relationships with employees that develop pride, creating a stable working environment and company spirit."
